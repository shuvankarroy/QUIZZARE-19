    Round Details

    Round 1: THE QUIZZICAL STRIKE

  • 35 questions based on current a affairs and general knowledge
  • time alloted: 20 mins
  • all questions carry equal marks.
  • in case of a tie breaker number of star questions will be counted
  • 26 teams qualify to the next round.

    • Round 2: ALPHABETICO

  • 26 teams compete to go to the next round 10 qualify
  • 26 questions with answers in alphabetical order.

      • Eg: Question 1- answer begins with 'A'.
      Question 2- answer begins with 'B' and so on

  • no negative marking.
  • 10 teams qualify to the next round.

    • Round 3: BOUNCE AND POUNCE

  • DIRECT QUESTION: correct answer gains +10 with no -ve markings for wrong answers.
  • POUNCE: once a direct question is thrown out to a team, the other teams get 10 seconds to pounce on it and lock their chance to answer it. For the teams who pounce, correct answers win them +10 whereas wrong answers come at a cost of -10 points .
  • BOUNCE: once the direct question team fails to answer correctly, the opportunity bounces on to the teams who have not pounced. Correct answers gain +5 with no -ve marking for wrong answers.
  • This round will be conducted in 2 phases. Two teams with the lowest score get eliminated after each phase.
  • 6 teams qualify to the final round.

    • Round 4: MIXED BAG

  • 6 teams compete for the top 3 positions.
  • Teams get to randomly choose their questions.
  • Each team gets to answer a total of 2 questions.
  • Correct answers win you +10 with no negative marking
  • Teams are eligible for bonus points on answering passed questions. No -ve marking.

    • Be prepared for surprise bumper questions to jump ahead of the other teams. Details to be revealed on the spot!
